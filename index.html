Welcome to Our Church Family

Grace Community Church is a place where everyone is welcome, regardless of where you are in your spiritual journey. We believe in the transformative power of God's love and strive to create a community where everyone can experience that love.

Our mission is to love God, love people, and make disciples. We accomplish this through authentic worship, Biblical teaching, genuine community, and compassionate service.
